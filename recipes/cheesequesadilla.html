Cheese Quesadilla

A super quick and melty treat, this Cheese Quesadilla is a go-to for when you need something easy and satisfying in minutes!

Ingredients

  • 1 tortilla
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese

Steps

  1. Heat a pan over medium
  2. Place tortilla in the pan, sprinkle cheese on top
  3. Fold tortilla in half and cook until golden brown on both sides
