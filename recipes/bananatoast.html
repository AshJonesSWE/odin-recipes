Banana Toast

A quick and delicious snack, this Peanut Butter Banana Toast combines creamy peanut butter with sweet banana slices on crispy toast for a satisfying bite. Perfect for breakfast or a midday treat!

Ingredients

  • 1 slice bread
  • 1 tablespoon peanut butter
  • 1 banana

Steps

  1. Toast the bread
  2. Spread peanut butter on the toast
  3. Slice the banana and place the slices on top
