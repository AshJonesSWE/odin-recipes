Avocado Toast

A creamy and nutritious snack, this Avocado Toast is simple yet satisfying, offering healthy fats and flavor in every bite.

Ingredients

  • 1 slice bread
  • 1/2 avocado
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Toast the bread
  2. Mash the avocado and spread it on the toast
  3. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
